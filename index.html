Chain of Change

The transistion from "Empire of Liberty" to New Imperialism

With this project, we present just a few of the connections between the varied events, people, ideals and places that make up the British Empire. By zeroing in on the domain as it transitions from an Empire of Liberty to New Imperialism, an overall picture emerges: We see a nation struggling to define itself in a new, global world. The Britons attempt to reconcile the ideals of freedom and liberty they hold so dear with the reality of a strong, imperial kingdom. The result is an interesting paradox.

The web presented displays just a fraction of the potential connections between these snippets of Empire. Studying a period of transition offers room to explore new possibilities and linkages. We urge you to draw your own ties between the events outlined, and others, to create a unique picture.

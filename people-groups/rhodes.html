Cecil Rhodes

Cecil Rhodes was a self-made man born in 1853. He perfectly embodied the ideals of free trade and of New Imperialism, as an entrepreneur and fierce English nationalist. He formed the De Beers diamond corporation and the entire state of Rhodesia in Africa. His harsh and racist treatment of his workers offers an ironic look at "The White Man's Burden."

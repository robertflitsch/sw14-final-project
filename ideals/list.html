Carrying the empire through this transition were the overarching ideals the Brits strove for. Priding themselves on an Empire of Liberty, the English needed to redefine themselves as New Imperialism took route. The did this and justified their actions by emphasizing new ideals such as free trade and "The White Man's Burden."
